University of North Alabama Universal Calendar

As students of the University of North Alabama, we saw a need for an all-in-one calendar. There is no resource on UNAâ€™s website that collectively shows all events, dates, and deadlines in one place for students to view. We decided to collect and display all calendar events divided into categories including: Registration, Academics, Student Clubs and Organizations, Sports, and Miscellaneous events. We decided that these calendars, although divided and somewhat organized, still cover a broad range of categories. There was still a need to further categorize these students events beyond the basic 5 categories that we picked out. We then created smaller, more specific calendar categories to accurately pinpoint a list of events that the student is looking for.
